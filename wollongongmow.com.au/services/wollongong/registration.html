Home Care Package, Self Managed NDIS & More


Locally approved provider of delivered meals for levels 1, 2, 3 & 4 Packages.

How Packages work
Once you are allocated a package you will need to state in your care plan that you wish to have meal services paid for out of your package. Your package provider will then need to contact Wollongong Meals on Wheels or alternatively you can contact our office on (02) 4226 5869 and we can get in contact with your provider to arrange a commencement date.

Please note: NDIS can be Self-Managed or Plan-Managed, we unfortunately at this time can not provide for Agency-Managed NDIS clients. 

 

Commonwealth Home Support Programme (CHSP)

The Commonwealth Home Support Programme (CHSP) is here to help senior Australians stay independent while living comfortably in their own homes.

Eligibility Criteria:

You may qualify for CHSP if:

  • You are 65 years or older (or 50 years or older for Indigenous Australians), OR
  • You are 50 years or older (or 45 years or older for Indigenous Australians) and are on a low income, homeless, or at risk of being homeless.

How to Get Started:

If you meet the eligibility criteria, we’re here to help! Simply call us at (02) 4226 5869 to register and start receiving meals tailored to your needs.

Anyone is eligible to order meals from Wollongong Meals on Wheels. We provide a meal delivery for anyone looking for a healthy, delicious, cost-effective meal service.

Additionally, Wollongong Meals on Wheels also provides meal delivery and social support services specifically for Wollongong seniors, younger people with a disability and their carers who are eligible for a Government funded service. To be eligible for these services, you must reside in the Wollongong area and meet at least one of the following criteria:
  • Aged 65 years or over
  • Aged 50 years or over and identify as Aboriginal or Torres Strait Islander
  • Have a registered disability
  • Primary carer of someone with a registered disability
 
 
HOW DO I REGISTER?
If you are 65 years or over (or 50 years or over for Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islanders), we require a referral from the government organisation, My Aged Care. To be referred to Wollongong Meals on Wheels, please call My Aged Care on 1800 200 422 or visit the My Aged Care website by clicking here. Alternatively, you can complete the form below or contact our friendly staff on (02) 4226 5869, and we can simply arrange the referral on your behalf. The process typically takes up to 48 hours, however, we can commence our service to you in the meantime if needed.

If you are under the age of 65 (or under 50 years for Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islanders) and living with – or caring for someone with – a registered disability, we generally require a referral from the NDIS. To be referred to Wollongong Meals on Wheels, please contact the NDIS on 1800 800 110, or visit the NDIS website by clicking here. Please feel free to contact our staff for more information on being referred to our service.
