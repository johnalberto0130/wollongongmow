About us
Wollongong Meals on Wheels has been a part of the Illawarra community for over 50 years, offering a range of flexible food options to assist people to remain well-nourished and living independently in the community.
Based in Wollongong, we provide meals to the Illawarra areas of Albion Park through to Thirroul.
 
Meals on Wheels is a trusted name and is familiar to communities throughout Australia. 
 
We Nourish:
 
Our meals are designed by dietitians and comply with the National Meal Guidelines published by Meals on Wheels Australia.
 
We care:
 
A friendly face at the door, a chance to check if you are alright. We are committed to providing our clients with the support required to remain in their homes and living independently.
 
We strengthen communities:
 
We rely on our volunteers to deliver our service and these same volunteers are the backbone of our Illawarra community.
